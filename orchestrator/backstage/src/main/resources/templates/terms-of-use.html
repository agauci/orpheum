Wi-Fi Usage Policy

Welcome to The Orpheum Malta's Wi-Fi service. By accessing and using this Wi-Fi network, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Permitted Use

The Wi-Fi network is provided for the convenience of our guests and is intended for general internet use, such as browsing, email, and social media. Users must ensure that their use of the network is lawful and respectful of others.

2. Prohibited Activities

Users must not engage in any activities that are illegal or that could harm or disrupt the network or other users. Prohibited activities include, but are not limited to:

  • Accessing or distributing unlawful, offensive, or obscene material.
  • Engaging in activities that infringe on intellectual property rights.
  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to other systems or networks.
  • Distributing viruses or other malicious software.
  • Conducting business or commercial activities that are not permitted.

3. Security

While we strive to provide a secure network, users are responsible for ensuring the security of their own devices. We recommend using up-to-date antivirus software and avoiding the transmission of sensitive information over the network.

4. Data Collection and Privacy

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we inform you that certain personal data may be collected when you access our Wi-Fi network. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., MAC address).
  • Connection logs (e.g., times and durations of access).
  • Browsing activity, if required by law.

This data is collected for the purposes of network management, security, and compliance with legal obligations. For more details on how we handle your personal data and your rights under GDPR, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for any loss or damage resulting from the use of this Wi-Fi network. This includes, but is not limited to, loss of data, security breaches, or damage to devices.

7. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using the Wi-Fi network, you acknowledge and agree to comply with the terms and conditions outlined in this Usage Policy. If you do not agree with any of the provisions, you must refrain from using the network. Accessing the network constitutes your acceptance of these terms.

We reserve the right to terminate or suspend access to the Wi-Fi network at any time, without notice, if we believe there has been a violation of this Usage Policy or if access is deemed harmful to the network, other users, or our property. Any violation of the prohibited activities or failure to comply with this policy may result in immediate termination of service.

In the event of a serious breach of this Usage Policy, such as illegal activities, cybercrime, or actions that threaten the security of the network or other users, we reserve the right to contact the relevant authorities.
